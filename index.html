The Visionary Behind Velvista

Meet Our Founder

"Every structure we build is a promise — to the land it stands on, and to the family it shelters."

Naresh RV founded Velvista Construction with a single conviction: that great construction is inseparable from great integrity. With over 15 years of hands-on experience in residential and commercial development across Tamil Nadu, he has personally overseen more than 250 projects from groundbreaking to handover.

A civil engineering graduate with a sharp eye for detail, Naresh built Velvista from the ground up — beginning with small residential projects in Chennai and growing into one of the region's most respected construction names. His philosophy of zero compromise on quality is embedded in every Velvista project.

Beyond construction, Naresh is passionate about sustainable building practices and is actively pushing Velvista toward fully green-certified developments by 2027.
